FABRIC

POLOS

Fabrics: single jersey, interlock, rib, pique (single, double, hon-eycomb), cotton/polyester/blends, performance.Washes: stone, enzyme, silicon, overdying.Finishes: peaching, mercersing, performance treatments.Style: prints, embroidery, appliqués, cut and sew.

DENIM

Fabrics: all weights from 6 to 15oz, regular, stretch, textured(crosshatch, broken, twill etc), slub.Washes: stone, enzyme, silicon, overdying.Finishes: whiskering, grinding, ozone treatment, shells/cor-als.Style: embroidery, appliqué, fashion fits/cuts.

TEES / TOPS

Fabrics: single jersey, interlock, waffle, rib, cotton and blends. gsm: 140 – 210 (jersey), 18-280 (rib & interlock, 180-260 (pique) Products: tees, vests, tanks, spaghetti, racer backs, long/ short/sleeveless. Style: prints, embroidery, appliqués, washed.

FLEECE

Fabrics: polar, three thread, french terry, cvc fleece, cvc terry, polyester, cotton & blends. Products: jackets, hoodies, round necks, pants, shorts, skirts, jogging suits, gilets. Style: prints, embroidery, appliqués, patches, badges/patches.

SHIRTS

Fabrics: plain, yarn dyed, poplin, dobbies, voiles, silks, cotton, poly cotton blend. Washes: poplin, oxford, twills, pinpoints. Products: dress, formal, casual. Style: all types of collars, cuffs, plackets.

JACKETS

Fabrics: twill, canvas, nylon, polyester, microfibre, cprd, flannel. Finishes: teflon, soil repellent, anti microbial, rain proof, bonded, anti static, water resistant. Products: casual, mountain, ski, parka, trench, sports, gilets. Style: hoods, special cuffs/hems, strings etc.

SWEATERS

Fabrics: cotton, acrylic, wool, all blends, cashmere, jacquards, intarsia, fancy yarns (eg slab, lurex, angora, pre twisting). Gauges: from 1.5gg up to 14gg. Products: pullovers, cardigans, vests, dresses, hoodies, ponchos, scarves, jackets. Style: embroidery, prints, washes.

BOTTOMS
